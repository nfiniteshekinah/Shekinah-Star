USER ▸ "Who is ASML and why does Nvidia need them?"
ASML is a Dutch company holding the world's only monopoly on Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) lithography — the machines that manufacture the most advanced semiconductors on Earth. Without ASML, TSMC cannot produce NVIDIA's chips. Without TSMC, there are no H100s, no Blackwells, no AI compute at scale. Every AI breakthrough in 2024-2026 traces back to a single factory in Eindhoven, Netherlands. China is banned from accessing ASML's most advanced machines by US export policy — making this one of the most geopolitically charged single points of failure in the global economy.
⚡ Star Signal: ASML is the most underappreciated AI infrastructure play. Any escalation in Taiwan Strait tension or US-China chip war directly shocks ASML's backlog and TSMC's production — watch this relationship as the defining geopolitical trade of 2026-2027.
