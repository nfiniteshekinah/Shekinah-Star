Privacy Policy
SARAHTOPIA LLC · SHEKINAHSTAR.IO · LAST UPDATED MARCH 2026

Plain English summary: We collect only what we need to run the platform. We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers. Your wallet data and trading information is yours. We are transparent about everything we store.

1. Who We Are

Shekinah Star is operated by Sarahtopia LLC, registered in Arizona. Contact us at sarah@shekinahstar.io.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address — used for portal login and communications
  • Wallet address — used for subscription verification and mirror trading
  • Agent key — used only for mirror trading, stored encrypted
  • Subscription tier and payment status via Superfluid
  • Risk preferences and trading configuration

Information collected automatically:

  • Chat conversation history with Star — used to personalize responses
  • Portal usage — which tabs you visit, when you log in
  • Trading activity mirrored from your Hyperliquid account
  • Basic server logs — IP addresses, browser type, timestamps

3. Star's Memory System

Star maintains a memory of your conversations and preferences to provide personalized service. This includes:

  • Your crypto experience level and financial goals
  • Your risk tolerance preferences
  • Topics you've discussed and questions you've asked
  • Your setup progress and wallet connection status

This memory is stored securely on our servers and is used only to improve your experience with Star. You may request deletion of your memory data at any time by contacting sarah@shekinahstar.io.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your subscription and portal access
  • To send you trade signals, weekly recaps, and platform updates
  • To execute mirror trades in your Hyperliquid account (if you have provided an agent key)
  • To verify your Superfluid subscription stream
  • To personalize Star's responses based on your history
  • To improve the platform and Star's trading strategies
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. What We Never Do

  • We never sell your personal data to third parties
  • We never share your data with advertisers
  • We never access your wallet beyond the agent key permissions you grant
  • We never store your private keys or seed phrases
  • We never share your email with other subscribers
  • We never use your data to trade against you

6. Data Sharing

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — AI providers (Anthropic, Groq, Google) process chat messages to generate responses. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Blockchain — wallet addresses and transactions are publicly visible on Arbitrum and Hyperliquid by nature of blockchain technology
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or court order

7. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • Agent keys are stored encrypted
  • All platform communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access to subscriber data is strictly limited
  • We do not store payment card information — all payments are on-chain via Superfluid

No system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Data Retention

  • Active subscriber data — retained while your account is active
  • Inactive accounts — retained indefinitely for reactivation purposes (soft delete only)
  • Chat history — retained to power Star's memory system
  • You may request complete deletion of your data at any time

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request that we stop processing your data
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights contact sarah@shekinahstar.io.

10. Cookies

We use session storage (not cookies) to maintain your portal login state. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking tools.

11. Children's Privacy

12. International Users

Shekinah Star is operated from the United States. If you access the platform from outside the US your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
