Star Navigation Component

This is the universal navigation bar. It appears on every page of ShekinahStar.io and adapts based on the subscriber's tier and login state.


Tier-aware: The tier badge changes color and label based on the logged-in subscriber's tier. Observer tiers see a Subscribe button. Sovereign+ see their tier displayed.


Status bar: Logged-in subscribers see a live price ticker below the nav showing BTC, ETH, SOL prices pulled from the Star API.


Dropdowns: Markets, Account, and About categories organize all pages without overwhelming the nav bar.


To integrate: Add the CSS and HTML from this component to the <head> and <body> of every Star page. Or use Flask's Jinja templating to include it via {% include 'star_nav.html' %}.
